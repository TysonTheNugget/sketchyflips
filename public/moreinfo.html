More Information about Coin Flip Game

IMPORTANT: This game is currently in BETA! Features may change, and there could be unexpected issues. Play at your own risk.

Welcome to the Coin Flip Game!

Coin Flip Game is an exciting blockchain-based game where two players stake their NFTs in a fair coin flip showdown. Powered by Gelato VRF for true randomness, the winner takes both NFTs! It's simple, thrilling, and fully on-chain.

How to Play

Here's a step-by-step guide on what you need to do to get started and enjoy the game:

  • Create a Game: Connect your wallet, select an NFT you own, and create a new game. Your NFT will be transferred to the contract as your stake.
  • Join a Game: Browse open games, choose one, select your NFT, and join. Both NFTs are now staked, and randomness is automatically requested via Gelato VRF.
  • Game Resolution: Once randomness is fulfilled, the winner is determined (50/50 chance), and both NFTs are transferred to the winner.
  • Cancel Options: If a game isn't joined within 1 hour, the creator can cancel and retrieve their NFT. If joined but not resolved after 24 hours, either player can cancel to get their NFTs back.
  • View Open Games: Check the list of available unjoined games to find one to join.

Risks and Important Notes

While the game is designed to be fair and secure, remember:

  • You are staking real NFTs—losing means you permanently transfer ownership to the winner.
  • Blockchain transactions can fail due to network congestion, gas fees, or wallet issues. Always double-check before confirming.
  • Gelato VRF ensures randomness, but delays in fulfillment could occur (though rare).
  • As this is beta, bugs might exist. Test with low-value NFTs first.
  • No refunds or reversals—blockchain is immutable. All actions are final.

Tips and Troubleshooting

- The contract is verified on ABScan, and VRF is provided by Gelato for transparency and fairness.
- If you encounter errors (e.g., transaction fails or UI glitches), try force-closing MetaMask or your Web3 app and retrying.
- Things can fail due to external factors, but the blockchain always remains true—check transaction status on ABScan.
- For best experience, use a stable connection and ensure your wallet has enough gas.
